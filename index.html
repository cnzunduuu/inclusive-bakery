Why Inclusive Design?

For individuals with Dyslexia, the font choice ('Lexend') minimises letter confusion and crowding. For thos with ADHD, the low contrast palette, large text, and organised layout reduces sensory overload, making the website easier to process and navigate.

Our mission is to prove that great design is inherently accessible design.
