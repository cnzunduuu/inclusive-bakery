Why Inclusive Design?

For individuals with Dyslexia, the font choice Lexend minimises letter confusion and crowding. For those with ADHD, the low-contrast palette, large text, and organised layout reduce sensory overload, making the website easier to process and navigate.

Our mission is to prove that great design is inherently accessible design.
